Instructions

This Application lets your children issue calls to your Telephone number/sip contact just by putting their face in front of the webcam without need to do anything else. The sound of a ringing phone will let them know the call is started.
  1. Click the Start button
  2. wait until the face detector is loaded
  3. Put your face in front of the camera
  4. Once your face is detected the call will be issued to the telephone number/sip contact you defined on settings
  5. Enjoy your call
  6. Once the call is finished the application will be ready for another call
